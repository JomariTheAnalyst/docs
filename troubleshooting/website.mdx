## Start with scope

Record the affected URL, time, device, region, deployment, user state, and exact symptom. Confirm the problem from a second browser or network before changing production.

## Check in order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check platform status">
    Review Vercel, Shopify, Sanity, Strapi hosting, Upstash, DNS, and business email status as relevant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check routing">
    Test `/`, `/ph`, the affected page, and `/api/health`. Confirm middleware is not applying maintenance mode unexpectedly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check deployment evidence">
    Match the live deployment to the expected commit and review Vercel runtime logs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identify the data owner">
    Decide whether the missing data belongs to Shopify, Sanity, Strapi, Redis, or static assets before editing content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply the safest recovery">
    Restore a known-good deployment or correct the failed external dependency. Avoid broad cache purges and unrelated configuration changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify customer paths">
    Check homepage, store, product, cart, checkout, content, contact, account entry, and mobile layout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Escalation information

Record errors, logs, deployment reference, affected routes, external status, actions, approvals, recovery time, and follow-up owner. Do not include customer, order, payment, or credential data.
