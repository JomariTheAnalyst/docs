## Catalog is empty or stale

1. Check Shopify service status and storefront logs.
2. Confirm the issue affects the expected store and environment.
3. Check product status, sales-channel availability, collection membership, and variant availability in Shopify Admin.
4. Review Storefront API errors without exposing tokens.
5. Check Redis and Shopify cache behavior.
6. Trigger approved revalidation only after upstream data is correct.

## Product is missing from the store

Check the product's publication state, `all-products` membership or automatic rules, brand collection handle, tags, product type, and availability. Confirm the storefront route uses the expected handle.

## Cart or checkout fails

<Steps>
  <Step title="Test an available variant">
    Use an approved product with stock. Confirm add, update, and remove actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Inspect the cart response">
    Check application logs for cart GraphQL errors and a missing `checkoutUrl`. Do not log customer tokens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Shopify status and settings">
    Verify store, checkout, market, publication, and payment configuration through an authorized administrator.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify hosted checkout">
    Confirm the browser redirects to Shopify. Never bypass hosted checkout or collect payment data in Next.js.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Escalate with

Record affected product or collection handles, route, error class, deployment, Shopify status, cache action, and recovery time. Do not record customer tokens or payment details.
