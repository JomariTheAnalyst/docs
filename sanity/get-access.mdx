A SixthGear administrator must invite you before you can open the CMS. The invitation is linked to one email address, so use the same account throughout the process.

<Info>
  Keep the invited email account open while you follow this guide. Do not forward the invitation or share your password with another person.
</Info>

## Before you begin

You need:

- Access to the email address that received the invitation
- The Sanity invitation email
- The sign-in method normally used for that account
- Approval from the SixthGear administrator who requested your access

<Warning>
  Using a different Google account or a different sign-in method can create a separate Sanity identity without access to the SixthGear project. Check the displayed account before every confirmation step.
</Warning>

## For the administrator: send the invitation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the SixthGear project in Sanity Manage">
    Open the correct SixthGear project and go to **Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the recipient">
    Check the exact email address the person will use. Do not send the invitation to a shared inbox unless shared access has been approved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the approved role">
    Assign only the access required for the person’s work. Available role names depend on the current Sanity plan and project configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send and verify the invitation">
    Send the invitation, confirm that it appears as pending, and tell the recipient which email address and sign-in method to use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The invitation email did not arrive">
    - Check **Spam**, **Junk**, **Promotions**, and **Updates**.
    - Search for `Sanity`.
    - Ask the administrator to confirm the invited email address.
    - Ask the administrator to review pending invitations and resend it if needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Sanity opens the wrong account">
    - Select **Switch accounts**.
    - Sign out of unrelated Sanity sessions.
    - Use a private browser window if account sessions remain mixed.
    - Sign in with the exact account that received the invitation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The invitation was accepted with the wrong account">
    - Stop before editing content.
    - Inform the SixthGear administrator.
    - Ask the administrator to remove the incorrect membership.
    - Send a new invitation to the correct account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The invitation expired">
    - Ask the administrator to review the pending invitation.
    - Use only the newest invitation email after it is resent.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The CMS opens, but editing is unavailable">
    - Confirm the assigned role.
    - Ask the administrator to review access in **Members**.
    - Do not use a shared administrator account to bypass permission limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Editing works, but publishing is unavailable">
    - Check the document for validation errors.
    - Confirm that all required fields are complete.
    - Ask the administrator to confirm that the assigned role allows publishing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="SixthGear CMS is blank or does not load">
    - Refresh the page once.
    - Wait a few minutes if the invitation was accepted recently.
    - Confirm that the correct account is signed in.
    - Try a private browser window.
    - Record the browser, time, address, and visible error before escalating.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Protect your account

Use your own named account. Never place passwords, API tokens, verification codes, recovery codes, private keys, customer information, or private project identifiers in this guide or its screenshots.
