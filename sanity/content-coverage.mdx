## Status meanings

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Ready** | The CMS section has been manually verified to update the intended storefront section. |
| **Under review** | The schema or document exists, but the complete editing and storefront behavior has not been approved. |
| **Managed elsewhere** | The information belongs in Shopify, application code, or another system. |
| **Moved** | The content remains editable in the CMS, but it belongs to a different page or document. |

<Warning>
  Do not copy Shopify product information into Sanity unless a documented CMS field is specifically intended for editorial presentation. Duplicating commerce data creates conflicting sources of truth.
</Warning>

## Before you edit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the status">
    Continue only when the target section is marked **Ready**, or when an approved administrator has authorized an **Under review** change for testing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the correct document">
    Confirm the document title and section before changing any field. For homepage content, use the intended **Homepage Settings** document.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make one approved change">
    Change only the requested fields. Avoid unrelated cleanup during the same publish action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and verify">
    Open the affected storefront page after publishing. Confirm the text, images, links, ordering, and mobile layout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Report a mismatch">
    Stop further edits when the wrong section changes, fallback content remains visible, or the update appears in a different document. Record the page, section, time, and screenshot for the website administrator.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Current completion blockers

The CMS documentation should not claim full operational completion until both items below are manually confirmed:

- Sanity Visual Editing reliably reflects draft and published changes in the intended preview workflow.
- The storefront and Studio use one confirmed canonical homepage document, with duplicate homepage records resolved or safely excluded.
