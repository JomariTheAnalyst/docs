The Device Directory connects company equipment to its business use and signed-in accounts. Use a short internal equipment name that staff can recognize without publishing a serial number.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Store equipment" icon="store">POS devices, payment terminals, printers, scanners, and inventory tablets.</Card>
  <Card title="Office equipment" icon="laptop">Company laptops and desktops used for administration or development.</Card>
  <Card title="Mobile equipment" icon="smartphone">Company phones and tablets used for communication, content, or account recovery.</Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
Use names such as **Store iMin POS** or **Inventory tablet**. Do not include serial numbers, device passwords, PINs, unlock patterns, recovery codes, or API tokens.
</Note>

| Equipment name | Equipment type | Platforms signed in | Account or email used | Used for |
| -------------- | -------------- | ------------------- | --------------------- | -------- |
| Store iMin POS | All-in-one POS | To verify | To verify | Current in-store checkout workflow; exact model and application use require verification |

<Warning>
Sixthgear ownership is confirmed only for an iMin all-in-one POS terminal. Do not add example devices as real assets until the equipment and company ownership are verified.
</Warning>

<Check>
For each verified device, record a short equipment name, type, signed-in platforms, company account, business use, assigned custodian, and approved removal or remote-sign-out process in the appropriate asset system.
</Check>
