This directory contains only business addresses found in active storefront source. Mailbox existence, forwarding, ownership, monitoring, MFA, recovery, and signed-in devices require verification in the business-email provider.

<Info>
A <Tooltip tip="A mailbox used by several approved people to read and respond to messages.">shared inbox</Tooltip> receives messages for a team. An <Tooltip tip="An address that forwards to another mailbox and may not have its own sign-in.">email alias</Tooltip> redirects mail. A <Tooltip tip="An identity used by an application to send messages; replies may not be monitored.">sender-only email</Tooltip> sends automated messages. An individual company email belongs to one named worker, while a system or service email is used by an application or integration.
</Info>
