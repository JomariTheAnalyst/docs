This section records which business accounts Sixthgear uses, why each account matters, who is responsible for it, and where it is signed in. It helps management grant access deliberately, recover accounts, and remove access when responsibilities change.

An *account directory* is the maintained list of company accounts and the people or roles responsible for them. It records non-secret facts such as login identity, monitoring responsibility, access authority, multifactor-authentication requirements, and signed-in devices.

<Warning>
Never add passwords, API tokens, recovery codes, private keys, device PINs, payment credentials, or environment-variable values to this documentation. `To verify` is safer than a guess.
</Warning>

## How to use this section

1. Open the directory that matches the account or device you need to review.
2. Confirm unknown information in the authoritative platform or with an authorized owner.
3. Record responsibility using a person's approved business role or name.
4. Store the actual credential only in the approved company password manager.
5. Review signed-in devices and remove access that is no longer required.
