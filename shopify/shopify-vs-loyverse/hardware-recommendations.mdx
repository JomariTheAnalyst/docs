## Preferred setup

Shopify POS runs on a supported iOS or Android phone or tablet. The standard application does not run on Windows or macOS computers.

Use a modular setup:

```text
iPad or supported Android tablet
+ Shopify-supported receipt printer
+ secure tablet stand
+ optional barcode scanner
+ optional compatible cash drawer
+ external Philippine payment terminal
```

Modular equipment is easier to verify, replace, upgrade, and support than an all-in-one terminal whose printer and peripherals depend on manufacturer-specific software.

## Tablet recommendation

The iPad A16 is the practical choice because it meets Shopify POS requirements without the cost of an iPad Pro. The 128GB Wi-Fi configuration is sufficient for a dedicated POS device, and its USB-C port, current operating-system support, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth provide flexible peripheral options.

Apple lists a one-year limited warranty and 90 days of complimentary technical support. Keep the device on a currently supported production iPadOS release and enable automatic Shopify POS updates.

## Receipt-printer recommendation

The exact recommended model is **Epson TM-m30III `C31CK50342`**, which includes Ethernet, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth in addition to USB interfaces. Shopify documents the TM-m30III as supported and supports 80mm paper for this printer.

For the selected iPad:

- Prefer **Ethernet** when the counter has a stable wired router connection. The printer joins the local network and avoids a one-device Bluetooth limit.
- Use **Bluetooth** for one iPad and one printer when wired networking is unavailable.
- Use **USB through the Shopify POS Hub** only if Sixthgear can source the supported hub; the hub was not verified from a Philippine seller.
- Do not select Wi-Fi for this iPad configuration because Shopify documents TM-m30III Wi-Fi setup for Android devices and POS Go, not iPad.
- Do not use direct USB-C with the iPad; Shopify documents direct USB-C for Android only.

The same exact model is listed by a second Philippine seller with a 24-month carry-in warranty, but that listing was out of stock. Require Grand Tech to confirm the applicable warranty, Epson Philippines supply, return terms, and order lead time in writing.

## Barcode scanner

Start with the iPad camera. Shopify POS can scan 1D and 2D product, receipt, and gift-card barcodes using the device camera, so a dedicated scanner is not required for initial use.

Add a scanner only when checkout volume is high, personnel scan many products continuously, or camera positioning slows service. Shopify supports the Bluetooth **Zebra DS2278**, but the Philippine listing reviewed at ₱18,900 was out of stock and another local supplier required an enquiry. Do not purchase it until a Philippine seller confirms stock, the exact kit, three-year scanner warranty, and Shopify POS pairing.

## Cash drawer

A cash drawer is needed only when Sixthgear accepts and stores cash at the counter. A connected drawer normally plugs into the receipt printer's drawer-kick port and opens after the approved cash transaction.

Do not purchase a generic drawer until the seller confirms compatibility with `C31CK50342`, the included cable wiring, the required 24V trigger, warranty, and return rights. A local generic drawer listing at ₱3,800 with an RJ11 cable and nine-month supplier warranty is a **budget reference only**, not an approved Shopify-supported model.

## External payment terminal

A separate Philippine card or QR terminal is required when Sixthgear accepts those payment methods and no approved Shopify integration is available.

1. Create the sale in Shopify POS.
2. Enter or confirm the amount on the external terminal.
3. Receive an approved payment result.
4. Mark the correct custom payment type in Shopify POS.
5. Confirm that the POS and terminal totals match.

The provider, terminal, acquisition or rental cost, transaction fees, settlement schedule, refund process, warranty, and support remain **To verify**.

## Do not purchase

- An iPad Pro when the standard iPad satisfies the POS requirement
- A Windows or macOS computer to run the standard Shopify POS application
- A Shopify card reader intended for an unsupported country
- A receipt printer based only on brand name rather than exact supported model
- A generic cash drawer before cable and voltage compatibility are confirmed
- A replacement all-in-one POS before the existing iMin test is complete
- Overseas-only or discontinued hardware without Philippine warranty and support
