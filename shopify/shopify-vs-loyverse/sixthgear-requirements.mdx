## Priority table

| Priority | Requirement | Evidence of completion |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mandatory | Keep the existing online storefront and Shopify commerce records operating | Online products, cart, checkout, customer, and order flows pass before and after the POS change |
| Mandatory | Use one authority for products, prices, inventory, customers, and orders | Staff know where each record is edited and a store sale updates the correct Shopify location |
| Mandatory | Complete cash, card, QR, refund, and reconciliation workflows | Test transactions match the POS, terminal, settlement, cash, and inventory records |
| Mandatory | Produce the required Philippine sales document | An accountant or BIR-qualified adviser approves the invoice, numbering, tax, and retention procedure |
| Mandatory | Use supported and stable hardware | The POS device, receipt printer, network, and required peripherals pass a complete store shift |
| Mandatory | Preserve required Loyverse information | Exports are complete, readable, securely stored, and assigned a retention owner |
| High | Continue selling during a realistic internet interruption | Approved offline transactions and reconnection complete without duplicates |
| High | Limit staff permissions | Named users can complete their duties without owner, billing, or unrelated administrative access |
| High | Keep support and recovery practical | Staff know who to contact and how to recover from app, printer, device, network, or payment failure |
| Preferred | Avoid a permanent Shopify-to-Loyverse connector | Store sales enter Shopify directly after the transition |

## Mandatory operating checks

<Check>Products and variants load with the approved names, prices, and barcodes.</Check>
<Check>A store sale updates the correct Shopify location and online availability.</Check>
<Check>Cash, card, QR, refund, void, return, and exchange records remain traceable.</Check>
<Check>Staff can print the approved receipt or invoice and reconcile the end-of-day totals.</Check>
<Check>The device and printer recover after a network or application interruption.</Check>
<Check>Store personnel can complete the workflow after training.</Check>

## Cost and support requirements

Management needs dated estimates for the Shopify plan and POS tier, hardware, payment-terminal charges, implementation, data export, training, supplies, warranty, replacement equipment, and staff reconciliation time. The lowest purchase price is not the lowest long-term cost when it creates duplicate maintenance or unsupported workarounds.

<Warning>
The exact iMin model, local payment provider, approved invoice workflow, cash-drawer model, scanner availability, and final implementation budget remain unresolved. Do not let an unresolved mandatory requirement become an undocumented manual workaround.
</Warning>
