Shopify controls discount eligibility and checkout calculation. Sanity controls visible announcement, popup, featured collection, and promo banner content used by the storefront.

## Before you create a campaign

Confirm the approved offer, start and end time, eligible products or collections, customer eligibility, usage limits, combinations, market restrictions, and campaign owner.

## Publish a coordinated campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure the Shopify discount">
    Use **Discounts** in Shopify Admin. Choose the approved discount type and review every eligibility and combination rule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare storefront messaging">
    Update the related Sanity marketing record only when approved copy and dates are available. Do not promise a discount that Shopify will not apply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the customer path">
    Verify product eligibility, cart behavior, hosted checkout calculation, excluded products, mobile content, and the end time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close the campaign">
    Confirm the discount and CMS messaging both stop at the approved time. Record results and unexpected redemptions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The repository does not document live discount types, automatic discounts, codes, schedules, or owners. Verify them in Shopify Admin before publishing campaign copy.
</Warning>
