## Purpose

Use this procedure for a single product or a small catalog update. Use [bulk import](/shopify/bulk-import) for reviewed CSV changes across many products.

## Prerequisites

- Authorized Shopify product access
- Approved product title, description, price, media and inventory information
- Product category, vendor, collection and tag decisions
- Required SixthGear metafield values

## Add a product

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the product editor">
    In Shopify Admin, open **Products** and select **Add product**. To update an item, select its product title instead.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter customer-facing information">
    Add the title, description, media, category, vendor, price, and search engine listing. Keep claims, fitment, and warranty details accurate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure options and stock">
    Add variants only when customers must choose an option such as size or color. Set each variant's SKU, price, availability, and inventory behavior.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize the product">
    Assign the intended collections and reviewed tags. Brand collections used by the storefront follow the `brand-*` handle convention.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete metafields">
    Fill the SixthGear fields that apply to the product. Use [Manage metafields](/shopify/metafields) for the exact keys read by code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and review">
    Select **Save**, then open the product on the storefront. Confirm its media, price, variants, stock, details, collection placement, and checkout path.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verify the result

- The product appears only in intended sales channels and collections.
- At least one available variant makes the item purchasable.
- Unavailable variants cannot be added to cart.
- Product details and metafields render without raw or malformed data.
- The product URL and search engine listing use the approved wording.

## Common problems

| Problem | Check |
| --- | --- |
| Product is missing | Product status, sales-channel availability and collection rules |
| Wrong brand filter | Collection handle must follow the storefront's `brand-*` convention |
| Product shows sold out | Variant availability and inventory policy |
| Details tab is empty | Required custom metafield values and types |
| Image is cropped poorly | Source image dimensions and focal composition |

Shopify's current product editor guidance is available in the [Shopify Help Center](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/products/add-update-products).
