## What Products controls

Shopify is the source of truth for products, variants, prices, inventory, and product availability. Product changes belong in Shopify Admin.

The headless Next.js storefront reads product data through Shopify APIs. Editing Horizon theme files does not update the live SixthGear storefront.

## Product workflow

{/* Component: Steps */}
<Steps>
  {/* Component: Step */}
  <Step title="Create or locate the product">
    Open an existing product or begin a new product record.
  </Step>

  {/* Component: Step */}
  <Step title="Complete the core product information">
    Add the approved title, description, and product details.
  </Step>

  {/* Component: Step */}
  <Step title="Upload and arrange media">
    Add product media and place it in the intended display order.
  </Step>

  {/* Component: Step */}
  <Step title="Configure variants, prices, and inventory">
    Set the product's options, variant values, pricing, and availability.
  </Step>

  {/* Component: Step */}
  <Step title="Add organization fields and metafields">
    Complete the verified fields used to classify and display the product.
  </Step>

  {/* Component: Step */}
  <Step title="Choose status and sales-channel availability">
    Review the product status and the channels where it is published.
  </Step>

  {/* Component: Step */}
  <Step title="Save the product">
    Save the record after reviewing the product configuration.
  </Step>

  {/* Component: Step */}
  <Step title="Verify it on the live SixthGear storefront">
    Confirm the product page, media, options, price, and availability behave as expected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important publishing rule

{/* Component: Warning */}
<Warning>
  An **Active** product may still be missing from the storefront when it is unavailable to the required sales channel. Verify both the product status and publishing availability. The exact headless sales channel required by the production storefront must be confirmed in Shopify Admin before the detailed publishing guide is finalized.
</Warning>
