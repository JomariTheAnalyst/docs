Shopify tags are searchable product labels. SixthGear also uses them in collection filters and related-product ranking.

## Tag rules

- Reuse an existing approved tag before creating a new spelling.
- Keep capitalization and punctuation consistent even though Shopify tag matching is not case-sensitive.
- Do not put passwords, supplier secrets, private notes, or customer data in tags.
- Check automatic collection rules before renaming or removing a tag.
- Confirm filter behavior after a tag change.

## Add or remove a tag

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the product">
    In Shopify Admin, open **Products** and select the product.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update product organization">
    In **Product organization**, select an existing tag or enter the approved new tag.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and verify">
    Select **Save**. Confirm the expected store filter, search result, collection rule, and related products.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
The recommendation helper scores products using shared tags and product type. A tag change can affect "you may also like" results even when no collection changes.
</Info>
