SixthGear CMS is the internal editing workspace for website content managed through Sanity. Use this section to get access and confirm that you can open the correct Studio before making any content changes.

<Warning>
  Some website sections are still being connected or reviewed. Access to the CMS does not mean every field currently updates the live website. Edit only sections that have been confirmed as ready.
</Warning>

## What you can do after access is approved

- Open the SixthGear Sanity project with your own account
- Review the content sections available to your assigned role
- Edit or publish approved content when instructed
- Confirm whether a document is in draft or published state
- Report missing sections or permission problems without sharing credentials

## Access checklist

- [ ] You received the Sanity invitation at the correct email address
- [ ] You accepted it using the same account that was invited
- [ ] SixthGear CMS opens without an access error
- [ ] The content sections required for your role are visible
- [ ] Your editing and publishing permissions match the access approved for you

<Info>
  Use your own named account. Do not share passwords, verification codes, recovery codes, or an administrator login with another person.
</Info>

## When to ask for support

Contact the SixthGear website administrator when the invitation was sent to the wrong email, the CMS opens under the wrong account, expected content sections are missing, or your role does not allow an approved task.
