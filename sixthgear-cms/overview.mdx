Sixthgear CMS refers to the Sanity-based editing system embedded in the storefront at `/studio`. The source also contains active Strapi imports, so CMS ownership must be documented feature by feature.

<Warning>
Do not remove or disable Strapi configuration until every active import and the production content migration have been verified.
</Warning>

This section is planned. Future pages will cover access, content types, editing, publishing, media, validation, and the confirmed boundary between Sanity and Strapi.
