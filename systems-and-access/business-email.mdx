Business email is a dependency for account recovery, billing notices, support, and platform ownership. The repository does not identify the email provider or administrators.

## Controls to establish

- Use business-controlled addresses for platform owners and billing contacts.
- Maintain at least two recovery-capable email administrators where policy allows.
- Require MFA for administrators and high-risk mailboxes.
- Keep recovery outside the mailbox being recovered.
- Review forwarding, aliases, delegates, retention, and inactive users.
- Protect domain email records during DNS changes.

## Verify access

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the provider and tenant">
    Confirm the authoritative email provider, business domain, billing account, and support plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review administrators">
    Confirm named super administrators, MFA, recovery methods, and offboarding controls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map critical mailboxes">
    Identify addresses used by Shopify, Sanity, GitHub, Vercel, the registrar, DNS, Resend, and the password manager.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test recovery safely">
    Test the provider's approved recovery process without disabling an active account. Record the test date and result, not recovery codes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Do not publish personal email addresses, mailbox contents, recovery codes, or identity documents in SixthGear Docs.
</Warning>
