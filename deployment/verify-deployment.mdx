## Prerequisites

- Deployment URL or verified production URL
- Deployment and commit reference
- Approved test product and checkout procedure
- Access to GitHub Actions and Vercel logs

## Verification checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm deployment identity">
    Match the deployment to the intended commit, branch, environment, and release request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check routing and layout">
    Open `/`, confirm redirect to `/ph`, then check navigation, footer, mobile layout, and a not-found page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check commerce">
    Open store, collection, search, and product pages. Confirm price, availability, variants, cart updates, and Shopify hosted checkout handoff.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check content systems">
    Review homepage, services, about, rider stories, First Gear Coffee, marketing content, and any changed Sanity or Strapi source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check operational routes">
    Confirm contact behavior, account entry, health route, sitemap, robots, canonical URLs, and intended maintenance state.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review automated tests">
    Confirm the production smoke job passes and review retained artifacts for retries or hidden instability.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Record the result

Record environment, deployment reference, commit, verifier, time, routes checked, test result, issues, and release decision. Do not record customer, order, payment, or credential data.
